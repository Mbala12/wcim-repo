Our Story

Winners Chapel Int'l Canada is a multi-ethnic church located in all provinces and territories of Canada, and a member of the Winners' Family worldwide. Every ministry, service, and outreach we run flows from one conviction: that the Word of God, rightly taught and lived out, transforms lives.

Our Foundation

Everything we build — our worship, our teaching, our outreach — rests on one unshakable foundation: the Word of God. It is this foundation that gives shape to the convictions we live by as a church.
